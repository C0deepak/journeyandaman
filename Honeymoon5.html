6 Nights & 7 Days

Overview

This is a perfect 5 Night 6 Days long weekend tour package for customers visiting Andaman and Nicobar Islands. Visit the top-rated islands in the Andaman archipelago during your visit. Indulge in cruise rides and water activities during this 4 days tour to Andaman. The tour comes inclusive of stay, transfers, cruise rides and sightseeing. A personal coordinator is assigned to you during your trip who is available for any ground assistance. This weekend tour of the Andaman Islands covers the top two islands in the Andaman archipelago. Both Port Blair and Havelock Islands are covered during this trip. The top places covered in this tour are

  • Cellular Jail
  • Light and Sound Show
  • Corbyns Cove Beach
  • Radhanagar Beach
  • Elephant Beach
  • Kalapathar Beach
  • Chidiyatapu Beach

In addition, we can customize this trip further to add some amazing water activities at Havelock Island. Making sure you weekend trip to the islands is a memorable one.

Some Gallery of the Tour

Day Plan

Airport Pickup: Check in to Hotel, After Lunch proceed to Corbyn Cove beach. Evening we visit Cellular Jail for the Light & Sound Show.

Trip to Baratang Island for visiting Limestone Caves & same day return to Port Blair.

Port Blair to Havelock: Morning is free for some optional leisure activities Scuba / Snorkeling/ High Sea Fishing(own cost) - Trip to Radhanagar beach & Kalapathar Beach. Night Stay.

Visit to Elephanta Beach, Trip to Neil Island, visit to Laxmanpur Beach, Natural Bridge. Night Stay. Special Candle Night Dinner.

Beach Visit for water sports, return to Port Blair. Evening visit City Market for shopping and collection of Souvenirs. Night Stay.

City Tour of Port Blair, Visit to Museum's, Chatham Saw Mill, Chidiyatapu Sunset View.
